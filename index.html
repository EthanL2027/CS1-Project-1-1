Nutella Icecream

Descreption

Nutella has a rich chocolate-hazelnut flavor that is sweet. It combines the taste of chocolate with great nutella flavor, resulting in a smooth and creamy ice cream enjoyed on its own or as a delicious addition to various foods such as ice cream.

Ingredients

  • Heavy whipping cream
  • Nutella
  • coffe extract
  • Sugar
  • Salt

Steps

  1. 3 cups heavy whipping cream
  2. 3/4 cup Nutella
  3. 1/2 teaspoon coffee extract
  4. 1/2 cup sugar
  5. 1/4 teaspoon salt

Cookies and cream Icecream

Descreption

Cookies and cream ice cream is a favorie of many ice cream engjoyer's. It is a cross from a vanilla base and cokkies blended in and big peices throughout the ice cream which makes it just incredible.

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Whole milk
  • Heavy cream
  • Vanilla extract
  • Salt
  • Oreos

Steps

  1. 3/4 cup sugar
  2. 1 cup whole milk
  3. 2 cups heavy cream
  4. 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  5. 1/4 teaspoon salt
  6. 1 sleeve Oreos

Mint choclate chip

Description

Mint choclate chip Ice cream is a delicius cross between mint ice cream and choclate. Mint ice cream is great on it's own but when you combine it with rich soft choclate chips it brings it to the next level

Ingredients

  • Heavy cream
  • Whole milk
  • Egg yolks
  • salt
  • Vanilla
  • Mint
  • Choclate
  • Sugar
  • Food coloring

Steps

  1. 2 cups heavy cream
  2. 1 cup whole milk
  3. 1/2 cup sugar
  4. 3 egg yolks
  5. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  6. 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  7. 2 drops of green coloring
  8. As much choclate as you want
