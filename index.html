Nutella Icecream

Descreption

Nutella has a rich chocolate-hazelnut flavor that is sweet and indulgent. It combines the taste of chocolate with great nutella flavor, resulting in a smooth and creamy ice cream enjoyed on its own or as a delicious addition to various foods such as ice cream.

Ingredients

  • Heavy whipping cream
  • Nutella
  • coffe extract
  • Sugar
  • Salt

Steps

  1. 3 cups heavy whipping cream
  2. 3/4 cup Nutella
  3. 1/2 teaspoon coffee extract
